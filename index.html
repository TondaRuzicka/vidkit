Why your files never leave

Your video stays on your device — start to finish

Every other "online video compressor" uploads your footage to a server you'll never see, does the work there, and sends it back. {{SITE_NAME}} doesn't have a server. The compression engine — your browser's built-in WebCodecs, or a WebAssembly build of FFmpeg as a fallback — runs against the file locally. Nothing is transmitted, stored, or logged.

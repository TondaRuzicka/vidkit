Why your files never leave your device

Every other "online video compressor" works the same way: you upload your video to their server, their server does the work, you download the result. That means waiting through a full upload, trusting a stranger's server with your video, and tools that throttle or watermark you to sell a subscription.

{{SITE_NAME}} uses the video engine already built into your browser (WebCodecs) to do the same work locally, hardware-accelerated, on your machine. The site you load is static files — there is no backend that could receive your video even by accident. Skeptical? Open your browser's developer tools, watch the Network tab while a video compresses, and count the zero bytes uploaded.
